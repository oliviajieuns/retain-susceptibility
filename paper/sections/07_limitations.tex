\section{Limitations}
\label{sec:limitations}

The profile is local, block-scoped, and setting-dependent. Loss-shake energy
depends on the parameter block, loss, precision, radius, and finite direction
bank; it can miss update sign and alignment, curvature, or off-block damage.
Its $2R$ pool-forward sweeps remove candidate-side reverse mode but do not
guarantee lower runtime. Hidden-state proximity indicates exposure, not
causal dependence. The heuristic mixture needs target-free development
outcomes and revalidation after changes to the model, request type, parent,
or budget. The method also covers only an enumerated prompt--answer universe
and primarily measures $\Delta$NLL. Guarded repair occurs after parent
unlearning and enforces a common feasibility region, not identical final
forgetting. It is neither a deletion certificate nor protection against
unenumerated failures, extraction, or relearning.
